SM Mall of Asia


SM Mall of Asia (MOA) is one of the largest and most popular shopping malls in the Philippines. Opened in 2006, it spans over 42 hectares and offers a wide range of retail, dining, entertainment, and leisure options. The mall features over 600 stores, including international brands, a wide selection of restaurants, cafes, and food courts, as well as various entertainment facilities like an IMAX theater, an ice skating rink, and a concert ground. Additionally, the mall is home to the SM by the Bay, a waterfront area that offers stunning views of Manila Bay, perfect for leisurely strolls and sunset watching.

Location: Seaside Boulevard, Pasay City, Metro Manila, Philippines





Pagasa Weather Station


The PAGASA Weather Station is part of the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA), the national meteorological and hydrological agency of the Philippines. It plays a crucial role in weather forecasting, climate monitoring, and disaster risk reduction.

Location: Subic, Olongapo, Philippines



Vessel Traffic Management System


The Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMS) in Subic Bay is a critical component of maritime safety and efficiency in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone, one of the Philippines' busiest maritime hubs. Managed by the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority (SBMA), the VTMS ensures the safe navigation of vessels and promotes efficient port operations.

Location: Subic, Olongapo, Philippines






QBO Innovation Hub


The QBO Innovation Hub is a collaborative space in Makati City, Philippines, designed to nurture and empower Filipino startups. A joint initiative by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST), IdeaSpace Foundation, J.P. Morgan, and the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), it aims to create a thriving startup ecosystem by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for collaboration.

Location: Makati City, Philippines



Pocketworx Inc. Iot Technologies Hub


Pocketworx Inc. IoT Technologies Hub is a leading innovation hub based in Makati, Philippines, specializing in Internet of Things (IoT) technologies and solutions. It serves as a collaborative space and business accelerator for companies and entrepreneurs focusing on IoT development, providing them with the resources and expertise needed to bring innovative ideas to life.

Location: Makati City, Philippines







Light Rail Transit Authority Line 2


The Light Rail Transit Line 2 (LRT-2) is one of the major transit lines of the Light Rail Transit System in Metro Manila, Philippines. It serves as a vital transportation route, connecting key areas in the city and surrounding regions, providing an efficient and affordable alternative to road-based transportation.

Location: Aurora Boulevard, Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines



National Museum of the Philippines


The National Museum of the Philippines is located in Manila, the capital city of the Philippines. It is an important institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the country's rich cultural heritage, history, and natural wonders.

Location: Padre Burgos Avenue, Manila, 1000 Metro Manila, Philippines
